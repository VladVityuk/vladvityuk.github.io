## Introduction
This Privacy Policy explains how **Фінансовий розвиток Vlad UA** (“we”, “our”, “us”) handles information when you use our application and its integrations with Meta (Facebook) products.

We do **not** store, retain, or process any personal data on our servers.

---

## Information We Collect
Our application does not collect or store any personal information.

When you interact with Facebook APIs, you may grant our app temporary access to:
- Basic profile data
- Page access tokens

This data is handled directly through Facebook’s secure API and is **not stored** by us in any form.

---

## How We Use Information
Any data received from Facebook is used **only in real time**, for the sole purpose of performing the actions you authorize, such as:
- Publishing posts to your Facebook Page

We do **not** store, log, or persist this data after the task is completed.

---

## Data Storage and Security
- We do **not** create or maintain databases containing user information.
- We do **not** store access tokens or profile details.
- All operations are performed directly via secure communication with Facebook’s API.
- After the operation completes, no data is retained.

---

## Data Retention and Deletion
Since no data is stored, there is no data to delete.

If you revoke permissions through your Facebook account settings, our application will immediately stop accessing your Page.

---

## Third-Party Services
Our app interacts only with:
- Facebook / Meta APIs

Usage of Facebook products is governed by Facebook’s Terms and Privacy Policy.

---

## Your Rights
You may:
- Revoke all permissions at any time in Facebook settings.
- Remove the app from your Facebook account.

Since we do not store any information, no further action is required on our side.
